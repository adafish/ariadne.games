About Us

 Ariadne Games is an independent game development studio cofounded by Amanda and Jeff Harper. Together, we have over 20 years of game dev experience and have worked on games with over a million players. We're fond of horror, mystery, strategy, and puzzles; our primary goal at Ariadne Games is to build games that delight former and current mall goths and kids who spent too much time in the woods.
